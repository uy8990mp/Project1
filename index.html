About Us

The Nepali Students Community (NeStCom) at Minnesota State University, Mankato strives to bring together individuals who share common interests in various aspects of Nepalese life and culture, in addition to promoting Nepalese culture at Minnesota State University, Mankato and the local community in Mankato. The association works together to organize events such as celebrating the graduation of Nepalese students, welcoming new Nepalese students, summer picnics, cultural programs.
We are a non-profit that fosters greater knowledge and understanding of literature, arts, cultures, values, manners, social customs, and other facts of diversities and similarities among Nepalese and Americans in Mankato, USA. We celebrate annual picnic and Dashain Tihar Mela with Americans, friends of Nepal and the Nepalese community in the greater Mankato and Twin Cities area. We also organize seminars, talk programs on socio-economics, socio-politics, geo-politics and human rights, cultural programs.If you are interested in learning about Nepal (people, culture, food, country) or you would like to get involved, please contact us at abc@gmail.com.
